About me

Goals

I am an ambitious aspiring full-stack developer with a primary focus on frontend development and user friendly design. I have solid knowledge of HTML and CSS, as well as a foundational understanding of JavaScript, but I strive to deepen my expertise in it. I am also set to learn React, Node.js, Express, and TypeScript. I am always pushing myself to improve, with the ultimate goal of becoming a highly skilled programmer.

What made me switch career?

My love for snowboarding and being out in nature—especially in the mountains—finally pushed me, after years of hesitation, to leave the big city of Stockholm. I took the leap, enrolled in a remote learning program, and in the begining of 2025 I moved to the mountains in Åre. After being tied to a physical workplace for so many years, I’ve developed a dream of working remotely. My hope is that after completing my education as a frontend developer, I’ll be able to make that dream a reality.
