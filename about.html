About me

Goals

I first got into coding when I worked closely with developers at my last job. I was amazed by what they could do and how their custom-built solutions made my work so much easier. It seemed really cool, and I wanted to be able to do that myself. So, I decided to take a programming course to see if it was something I’d enjoy. Turns out, I loved it! Even though I’m still pretty new to coding, I’m excited to keep learning and growing. My main interest is in frontend development, but I’m also really intrigued by backend and embedded systems. I know I’ve got a long way to go, but I’m excited to learn and my goal is to become a highly skilled programmer.

What made me switch career?

My love for snowboarding and being out in nature—especially in the mountains—finally pushed me, after years of hesitation, to leave the big city of Stockholm. I took the leap, enrolled in a remote learning program, and in the begining of 2025 I moved to the mountains in Åre. After being tied to a physical workplace for so many years, I’ve developed a dream of working remotely. My hope is that after completing my education as a frontend developer, I’ll be able to make that dream a reality.
